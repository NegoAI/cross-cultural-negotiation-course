- **Citation density and accuracy are high; zero fabrications found.** 80+ distinct sources. Six non-canonical citations spot-checked on the web — all real and accurately titled (see citation table). Canonical course texts (Hofstede, Hall, Meyer, GLOBE/House, Trompenaars, Salacuse) recur correctly.

*Hardest-test feature earning the 90+ band:* zero fabricated citations across a thorough non-canonical spot-check, combined with chunks that carry explanation + worked example + framework-as-hypothesis limits rather than framework names. (Held just below the top of the band because some IVR/Indulgence material is thinner than PDI/IDV/UAI — the KB itself flags this.)
